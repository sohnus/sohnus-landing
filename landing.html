What we're fixing

You're good at your job.
The system doesn't see it.

You've spent years getting good at this. Nobody outside your workplace knows it.

There's no profile for people who work with their hands. No way to show what you've built, what you know, or how long you've been doing it. To everyone else, you have to start over and prove yourself again.

The job was posted at 7am. By the time your shift ended, it was gone.

Work opportunities live in WhatsApp chats and Facebook groups. They move fast. When you do reply, you're one name out of fifty, and nobody in that thread knows who you are. No history. No reputation. Just another message in a chat that's already moved on.

They called while you were working. Then they stopped calling.

You get a callback, finally. They want to interview you Wednesday at 2pm. You're on shift. You explain. They say they'll find another time. You follow up. Nothing. By then they've moved on to someone else.

What Sohnus is

Three things workers were
never given. We're building all of them.

We're being upfront, this is our roadmap. What we're building first, what comes next, and where we're going.

Phase 1:Identity & Community

A professional home for workers

A profile that shows who you are: your skills, your experience, proof of what you've done. A feed where you connect with workers in your trade, share what you know, and learn from people who've been where you are.

Phase 2:AI Agent

An AI agent that works when you can't

While you're on shift, your AI agent handles the job search moments you'd otherwise miss, answering employer questions, managing scheduling, responding on your behalf. No more lost opportunities because you were doing your job.

Phase 3:Jobs & Matching

Jobs that match who you actually are

We match roles to the right workers by real skills and real experience, not by CV. Workers apply using their Sohnus profile, and employers use your AI agent to properly assess who you are before they even meet you.
